Teaching philosophy

My teaching integrates open-source tools, real-world datasets, and hands-on coding from day one. All courses use Jupyter Notebooks and follow FAIR data principles — making science reproducible, accessible, and reusable. Students work with real precipitation, runoff, and climate datasets from the Andes and beyond.
